Our Menus

Big Brew offers a diverse menu of indulgent beverages focused on milk tea, frappes, and fruit tea.

From classic milk tea flavors like taro and okinawa to creative options like matcha red bean, there is something for every taste.

With refreshing fruit tea combinations and tempting frappe flavors such as caramel macch and moca, Big Brew invites you to explore and enjoy the perfect blend of flavors in every sip.
